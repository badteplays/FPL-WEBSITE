How our company started

We are a 10-person app studio that gets hired to build apps. Unlike solo marketplaces, we work as a coordinated team — product, design, engineering and QA — to deliver paid projects for clients.

We take client briefs, split work across our team, and ship reliable apps that classrooms and businesses use.

Timeline

2019

Founders collaborate

A small group noticed the same problem popping up in classrooms and decided to build a solution together.

They sketched ideas, validated basic flows with peers, and committed to a lightweight prototype to test real usage.

2020

Prototype & testing

An initial prototype was built and shared with a few teachers for feedback.

Feedback highlighted the most valuable workflows and guided simple, high-impact improvements to usability and onboarding.

2023

Beta & pilots

We ran a closed beta with a handful of classrooms to validate the app in real lessons.

Pilot teachers provided actionable feedback that improved lesson flows, reporting, and reliability ahead of a broader release.

2024

Public launch / Today

We launched a focused product aimed at classrooms and small organizations, emphasizing simplicity and support.

Today the studio takes client-paid projects and continues iterating based on teacher and client needs — delivering apps as a coordinated team.
